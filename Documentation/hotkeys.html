Replay Hotkeys

  • Delete selected: backspace or del
  • Cut/Copy/Paste: ctrl-x/ctrl-c/ctrl-v
  • Reorder up/down: ctrl-up/ctrl-down
    • NOTE: Mac users should check Apple Menu > System Preferences > Mission Control > Keyboard and Mouse Shortcuts, as ctrl+up/down may be overridden by default.
  • Reorder top/bottom: ctrl-shift-up/ctrl-shift-down
